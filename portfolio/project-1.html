Description du projet

Crown is a web template that I created targeting the restaurant and food industry for people to use for their own restaurant business and build trust and engagement with their customers by providing them with a better User Interface on the Web.

By taking User Interface ideas by visiting different types of websites of the same industry and even visited platforms like dribble to get inspiration for building a good User Interface that will bring great results.
